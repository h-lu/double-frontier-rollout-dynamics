Interpretation:
- The upper-right edge does not look like a broad smooth taper of the strong period-2 tongue.
- Instead, the large-amplitude period-2 state can collapse sharply over a small parameter step:
  - example: theta0 = -4.84, E = 1.04 -> 1.05
  - burst score drops from 1.624630 to 0.118698
  - p2 drops from 2.779549 to 1.735479
- A similar pattern appears at theta0 = -4.83:
  - E = 1.02 is strongly period-2
  - E = 1.03 still keeps a period-2 signature, but at much lower amplitude
- So the edge is best described as:
  1. a sharp loss of the strong large-amplitude period-2 relaxation state
  2. often followed by a weaker small-amplitude alternating remnant
  3. not an obvious transition into higher-period or irregular bursting in the tested points

Working conclusion:
- The strong tongue does not simply "fade out" continuously everywhere.
- Near the fragile corner, it often appears to jump from strong large-amplitude period-2 modulation to a weaker residual alternating regime over a very small parameter change.
